# Cash, ATMs, SIM/eSIM & Wi‑Fi in Kyoto

## TL;DR

- Cards accepted widely; keep ¥10,000–¥20,000 cash for small shrines/shops.
- ATMs: 7‑Eleven, Japan Post, FamilyMart are foreign‑card friendly.
- eSIM is easiest; airport counters sell physical SIMs; pocket Wi‑Fi for groups.

## Credit Cards & Cash

- Visa/Mastercard widely accepted; AmEx/JCB common; Discover limited.
- Tipping isn’t expected; no need to add tips on card.

## ATMs

- Use 7‑Eleven/JP Post/FamilyMart; check fees and daily limits.
- Withdraw in larger chunks to reduce per‑withdrawal fees.

## Mobile Data: eSIM/SIM/Wi‑Fi

- eSIM: buy online before arrival; activate on Wi‑Fi; keep QR handy.
- Physical SIM: airport kiosks and big electronics stores.
- Pocket Wi‑Fi: good for families; confirm pickup/return location.

## Connectivity Tips

- Keep a power bank; enable offline maps; café Wi‑Fi is inconsistent.
